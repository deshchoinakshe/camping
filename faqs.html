FAQs

Questions? Look here.

What level of camping experience do I need to join your tours?

Our tours cater to all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned campers. Our expert guides provide support and instruction as needed.

What safety measures are in place during tours?

Safety is our top priority. We provide safety briefings, first-aid kits, and follow Leave No Trace principles.

What should I pack for a camping trip?

We provide packing lists for each tour. Generally, essentials include clothing, suitable footwear, a sleeping bag, toiletries, and personal items.

Are meals provided during the tours?

Meal options vary by tour. Some tours include meals prepared by our guides, while others require you to bring your food. Details are provided in tour descriptions.

How large are tour groups?

Group sizes vary but are typically small to ensure a personalized experience. Specific group sizes are mentioned in tour descriptions.

Are pets allowed on your tours?

Pets are generally not allowed on our tours due to the potential impact on wildlife and other participants. Service animals may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Is camping equipment provided, or should I bring my own?

We offer equipment rental options, but you're welcome to bring your gear. Equipment availability varies by tour, so please check tour details.

Do you offer group discounts or custom tours?

Yes, we offer group discounts and can create custom tours for special occasions or specific interests. Contact us for details.
